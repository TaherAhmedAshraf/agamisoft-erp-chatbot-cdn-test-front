üöÄ Quick Setup

Add the chat widget to your website in just 3 simple steps:

Step 1: Include Socket.IO

<script src="https://cdn.socket.io/4.7.4/socket.io.min.js"></script>

Step 2: Include Chat Widget Files

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://your-cdn.com/chat-widget.css"> <script src="https://your-cdn.com/chat-widget.js"></script>

Step 3: Initialize the Widget

<script> ChatWidget.init({ serverUrl: 'http://your-backend-url', position: 'bottom-right', theme: 'default', companyName: 'Your Company', welcomeMessage: 'Hello! How can we help you today?' }); </script>
